About ErgoMatrix

Built for the future of work.

ErgoMatrix is on a mission to rescue millions of desk workers from chronic pain and preventable spinal damage by turning every ordinary chair into an intelligent posture trainer.

Our origin story

Born from the realities of desk work

ErgoMatrix was founded by a team that has watched long hours at a desk silently erode health, focus, and confidence. Instead of another cushion or static chair, we designed an active system that senses posture in real time and responds with gentle, corrective cues.

  • Eye-Q Sensor measures your true head-to-screen distance with precision optics.
  • Mesh-Up translates bad posture into soft, localized haptics that guide you upright.
  • The result is a full feedback loop that gradually retrains how you sit every day.
What makes us different

Not just ergonomic furniture

Most ergonomic solutions cushion the problem; ErgoMatrix actively intervenes. By combining sensing, haptics, and data, Posture Pro behaves more like a coach than a chair, nudging you into better form before strain builds.

  • Transforms any compatible office chair into a smart ergonomic trainer.
  • Helps reduce chronic pain risk while protecting deep focus and energy.
  • Designed for coders, creatives, founders, and teams who live at a desk.
